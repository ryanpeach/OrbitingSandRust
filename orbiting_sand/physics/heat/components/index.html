Most of the units of measure as well as bevy components for heat related physics.

Structs

  • Area
    The area of a system in square meters.
  • HeatCapacity
    The amount of heat energy required to raise the temperature of a system. Measured in joules per degree celsius.
  • HeatEnergy
    The amount of heat energy in a system. Measured in joules.
  • Length
    The length of a system in meters.
  • SpecificHeat
    The amount of heat energy required to raise 1 kilogram of the substance by one degree. Measured in joules per kilogram per degree celsius.
  • ThermalConductivity
    Thermal Conductivity The ability of a material to conduct heat. Measured in watts per meter per degree celsius.
  • ThermodynamicTemperature
    The temperature of a system. Measured in Kelvin. This should be represented in “real” units, physically similar to the real world.
